The Users

The users that we determined our target were over the age of 30, most interested in local or widespread major news stories or information. Because of this, we focused on finding local most wanted and local news, condensing the navigation/reducing repetitive calls to action, streamlining the page content to be more visually navigable, and making it an immediately visible one-click process to get to the “submit a tip” page.
